RECEPTION & OFFICE ASSISTANT

Key Responsibilities:

Greet and welcome guests and direct visitors to the appropriate person and office;
Answer screen and forward incoming phone calls;
Provide basic and accurate information in-person and via phone/email;
Receive sort and distribute daily mail/deliveries;
Maintain office security by following safety procedures and controlling access via the reception desk (monitor logbook issue visitor badges);
Order front office supplies and keep inventory of stock;
Keep updated records of office expenses and costs;
Maintain trusting relationships with suppliers customers and colleagues;
Resolve office-related malfunctions and respond to requests or issues;
Arrange meetings by reserving rooms and managing refreshments;
Record and keep records of invoices contracts and other documents (powers certificates addresses etc.) received at the reception;
Archiving the contracts in the company depository
Assist in the preparation of preliminary documents in order to renew / extend the contract with the suppliers of the admin department at this stage;
Monitor the general e-mail address and respond to requests together with the departments concerned;
Other administrative tasks.

The contract is for 6-month period

Requirements:

Independence in the performance of tasks and a proactive approach
Organizational skills communicativeness diligence attention to detail flexibility
English language – intermediate
Romanian - native speakers
Experience on similar position
