Who We Are

KPMG is one of the world’s leading providers of Audit Tax and Advisory services.

At KPMG we run our firm just as effectively as we support and strengthen our clients’ businesses. That means hiring talented people to help develop and manage our brand publish our thought leadership control our finances run our IT infrastructure and support our professionals in the field.

Why KPMG?

We place significant emphasis on our people and their life context fostering the KPMG culture with our values energy and trust.

Being part of KPMG means having the chance to work with many supportive and talented people who are willing to share their experience & expertise with you. We also put technology at the heart of our daily activities by exploring new innovative approaches and encouraging our people to be groundbreaking in their thinking.

Our aim is to attract talented graduates with a diverse range of academic backgrounds to become part of KPMG in one of our Tax teams such as: Corporate Tax and Mergers & Acquisitions Transfer Pricing Tax VAT Tax Accounting or Tax Payroll.

As part of our team you will have the opportunity to get involved in challenging projects acquire expertise while working in multiple industries work with experienced advisory professionals and improve your skills knowledge and capabilities by on-the-job and structured training. In this role you will get to know what it means to be a professional consultant and you be able to pursue a career path that best matches your personality skills and interests.

Candidate’s Profile

Making the right career choice is important. Finding the right place to do that is essential.

Are you a young professional looking for a great start to your career? #comeasyouare We are Open for Talent!

Requirements

Recent university graduate (Romanian or foreign universities) in Economics Finance Technology Mathematics IT Law or a related discipline with a strong academic record
Fluency in both Romanian and English
Good communication and presentation skills both verbally and in writing
Strong analytical and quantitative skills
Curiosity and a lifelong learning mindset
Out of the box thinking
EQ
To be tech-savvy
Good judgment & decision-making skills
Business-oriented attitude creativity and assertiveness
To be a good team player who takes ownership of their actions.

Your role

You will become an integral part of the engagement team creating good contacts and developing relationships with client teams and the wider business community.
You will develop an understanding of clients’ systems and processes. You will apply knowledge gained through training and on-the-job experience to undertake risk based assessments of the level of work required.
You will own assigned work and assist more senior members of the team.
You will seek to embed technology and data analytics into the work process.
You will participate in social and networking events to develop your knowledge and contacts within KPMG.

Career Development

You will be supported in your career progression through performance management formal and informal training personal development programs etc.
You will have access to learning & development programs.
You will have access to mobility programs and other career opportunities within the firm.

KPMG in Romania offers you:

The chance to work in a professional environment exceptional opportunities for career growth as well as a strong learning and development culture. We’ll do everything we can to help you fulfill your potential through various training courses and additional professional qualifications.
A fast paced environment and a diverse team with great mentoring opportunities.
An attractive remuneration package.
A combination of great jobs to work on.
Great colleagues to work with.
Good career advancement opportunities.
A flexible workplace and work environment.

Working at KPMG is also about happy people and an internal culture that supports employees in following their passions through various activities and events. We also have numerous benefits to support your health a flexible working schedule and many more advantages.
