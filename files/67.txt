We are seeking an experienced person for

Junior HR Operations Specialist

Business Context And Main Purpose Of The Job

The Junior HR Operations Specialist will be for effectively delivering HR administrative tasks and supporting the HR professionals in Romania.

Main Accountabilities
Perform various HR administrative tasks efficiently and in a timely manner.
Manage employee lifecycle preparing the necessary documents starting from hiring job changes until termination of individual employment contract monitoring the entire process for compliance with local laws and Company policies & procedures
Responsible for updating Revisal (Labor system) and personal files
Act as first point of contact with Labor Office Unemployment Agency etc
Data maintenance in the Workday and other HR systems
Collecting Admin data (New Hires Changes Leavers) for Payroll
Providing information to the employees on HR related aspects including release any document requested by employees
Administering employees' benefits
Maintaining and updating documents related to HR Administration processes
Key Skills And Qualifications
Good communication skills (verbal and written) in Romanian and English
1-2 years experience on a similar position
Excellent knowledge of Romanian labor legislation including holding the certificate of human resources inspector
Good knowledge of HR related functions and processes Experience with HR systems (e.g. Workday ServiceNow Una)
Proactive approach to problem solving and ability to identify areas of improvement in processes and team ways of working
Very good analytical and MS Office skills (mail merge pivot tables ppt)
Well-organized accurate and attentive to details
Team player open personality proactive attitude
Our Company Offers
Continuous training
Progressive work experience and steadily increasing responsibility
International career opportunities
Competitive remuneration
Working in a hybrid model (possibility to work from home and Bucharest office)
